SHRI BHUPINDER
SINGH JAUHAR

FOUNDER
(1933-2021)

Leading Heads Behind Jamna Auto

Mr Bhupinder Singh Jauhar is the spirit behind the Jamna Group. He started the spring business in 1954 in a small shop in Yamuna Nagar which has now acquired global recognition as one of the major producer of Leaf and Parabolic Springs in the world. He worked tirelessly to turn his vision into a reality. He was actively associated with leading educational institutions in Yamuna Nagar and Ambala. He was an innovative Industrialist with vast experience leveraging engineering methodologies to deliver highly effective and creative solutions in Leaf Spring for various range of commercial vehicles.
His vision, values and guidance will continue to take the company to new frontiers of success.





"COMPLETE SUSPENSION SOLUTION PROVIDERS"

JAI manufactures high-quality suspension solutionsfor a wide range of commercial vehicles. Our product range includes Leaf Springs, Parabolic Springs, Lift Axles & Air Suspensions. JAI is a well known brand in automotive Industry catering to all OEM's, Replacement Market and Export Market. For Springs – we have the highest annual capacity of over 3,00,000 MT.
